Shipping Policy

Free Shipping: We are pleased to offer complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the continental United States.

Processing Time

At Artisan Essentials, we treat every order with the care it deserves. Please allow 1-3 business days for our team to hand-pack and dispatch your items. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the following business day.

Shipping Rates & Estimates

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): $5.99 (Free for orders $75+)
  • Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day): Calculated at checkout

International Shipping

We currently ship to select countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and weight. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes incurred.

Tracking Your Order

Once your artistry tools have left our facility, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking number. Please allow 24 hours for the carrier to update the tracking status.

Packaging

We believe the unboxing experience is part of the creative process. Your items will arrive securely packaged in eco-friendly materials, ensuring they reach you in pristine condition.
