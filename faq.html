Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Are your products authentic?
Absolutely. We are an authorized retailer for all brands we carry, including Moleskine, Pilot, and Faber-Castell. Every item is 100% authentic and sourced directly from manufacturers.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to many countries globally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location and will be calculated at checkout. Please note that customs duties may apply.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also view your order status by logging into your account on our website.
Can I modify my order after placing it?
We process orders very quickly. If you need to make changes, please contact us within 1 hour of placing your order. Once it has been packed, we cannot modify it.
Do you offer bulk discounts for artists?
Yes, we support the art community! Please contact our sales team via the Contact page for information regarding bulk orders for studios, schools, or workshops.
