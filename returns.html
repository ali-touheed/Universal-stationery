Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your artistic tools. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help ensuring your creative journey remains uninterrupted.

30-Day Return Policy

You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in the original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

For safety and quality assurance, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened ink bottles or cartridges.
  • Used brush pens or nibs.
  • Personalized or engraved items.
  • Gift cards.

How to Start a Return

1

Contact our support team with your order ID.

2

Receive a prepaid shipping label via email.

3

Pack your items and drop them off.

Refunds

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you on the status of your refund. If approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges

Need a different nib size or notebook color? The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.
